Welcome to audio bliss @ HACIENDAStudios

Hacienda is a high end recording studio based in the heart of Manchester. We offer services such as recording, mixing and mastering. With a wealth of experience and a glorious collection of vintage/modern gear we will get your tracks sounding professional and ready for commercial release.
